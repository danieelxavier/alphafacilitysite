Our Company

Alpha Facility Group is a Maritime Agencyfounded in 2002, headquartered in Amazonas, which provides assistance in loading and unloading ships and assists port operators in issuing documents.

We guarantee that our clients are treated with total excellence in our services performed in the Brazilian ports. During these years, we have worked and won the professional trust of great shipowners from Brazil, Singapore, Greece, India and Philippines.

In addition to the conventional agency, we also offer support for the exchange of crew, food, supplies, naval repairs, medical care, hold cleaning, among others (Protector Agent Service).

We provide maritime agency services such as full agent, charterer’s agent, owner’s agent, protecting agent and advisory agents for various types of ships and national maritime cargoes (long range navigation, small and long cabotage) and international, such as passenger, Containers, bulk carrier, tanks, general cargo and offshore.

We also work in the commercial and operational assistance to vessels calling brasilian ports of regular fleets (line), non regular (tramp) and in the elaboration of documentation relative to all these types of ships.

Our employees are bilingual and qualified to offer differentiated service to customers, operators operators and charterers, ensuring cost-optimization. Quality and speed confirmed by the most demanding inspectors: the customer!

MARITIME AGENCY

Contracting and coordination of operational services (pilotage, tugs, boats, etc.), ensuring support for the maneuvering of ships in ports and coordination in on-board operational services with the port operator.

Clearance of port authorities and assistance by issuing certificates (Harbor Master, Federal Police, Customs and Health).

Release of vessel towards local authorities, rectifying defi ciency (Flag/Port State Control), Statement of Compliance (Inspection/VIA SIRE).

Crew change management. Crew change orders, crew registry toward competent authority, monitoring all procedures regarding crew members.

CUSTOMS CLEARANCE

Registration of loads in the Siscomex system.
Monitoring and issuing cargo documents. Issue of necessary documentation for operation, such as Mates, Statement of Facts, etc.

TRANSPORTATION

We offer bilingual drivers on the cars, vans, minibuses or any other shore transportation. Includes crew supporting to doctors and hospitals, or other shore needs. In addition boat service for ships at the anchorage.

REPAIR AND CLEANING SERVICE

Paintings and repairs vessels, including washing and preparation of holds for loading of grains and others.

Container repair and washing. Structural repairs in containers of steel, fiber, aluminum, dry box aluminum, reefers, stainless steel, flat-rack, open top, open half and tank, according to the Institute of International Container Lessors.

MONITORING OF PORT OPERATION

We are the link between the ship and the port. We got the boats.

OTHER SERVICES
